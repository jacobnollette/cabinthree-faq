- Please do not plug a car into the outside wall socket - we have a tesla universal charger, and that should cover basically all recent cars
- We are on peak metering, so please try to charge during these times
    - monday - thursday: charge after 10pm, or before 4pm
    - friday - after 10pm to monday at 4pm
    - Any federal (bank) holiday: midnight to midnight
- Electrical costs
    - On Peak (4 pm – 10 pm Weekdays) 20.4¢/kWh
    - Off Peak (All other times) 8¢/kWh
- There are superchargers in Bemidji; there are also CCS chargers in Walker (casino) and Detroit Lakes (Ford dealership)
- <u>***Do NOT step on or roll anything over the charging cable - please hang up the charging cable when not in use - please do no bend or knot the cable, and untangle it so it hangs loosly.***</u>
- If the charging cable is hanging, plug it in either to a car, or to its base
