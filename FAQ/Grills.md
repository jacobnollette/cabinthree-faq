- Don’t use the smoker - its messy.
- Mindful of scraping down the griddle - keep the scraper flat, and try not to dig the corners into the seasoning (advice 🙂 )
- If run out of gas, but a new one (if possible), and let Jacob know to Venmo you money 🙂
- Griddle tools live on the deck, and they are dishwasher safe — there may be some in the deck box
- Use lots of vegetable (high heat) oil for the griddle
- Make sure to reduce the standing oil on the griddle with heat, or paper towels before packing it up
- Make sure to replace and clean the drip tray in the back of the griddle — to keep critters away and grease off deck
